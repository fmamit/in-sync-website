How It Works

From enquiry to disbursal

Step 01

Fill out a simple application with your basic business details and KYC information. The entire process is digital - no branch visits, no phone calls, no paperwork. You'll receive an acknowledgement within minutes.
Step 02

Upload your business financials, KYC documents, and collateral details through our secure portal. We accept scanned copies - no originals needed, no couriers, no waiting.
Step 03

Our credit team evaluates your application using clearly-defined criteria. You're kept informed at every stage. No black boxes, no unexplained rejections.
Step 04

Upon approval, loan amount is credited directly to your bank account. Average turnaround from application to disbursal is just 7 working days.
For secured loans, we accept property (residential or commercial), plant & machinery, and other fixed assets as collateral. Equipment finance is secured against the equipment being financed. Supply chain finance may be partially secured or backed by receivables.
Business must be registered in India with a minimum vintage of 1 year. We serve sole proprietorships, partnerships, LLPs, and private limited companies. Specific criteria vary by product - use our eligibility checker for instant results.
