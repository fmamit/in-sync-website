How It Works

From enquiry to disbursal

Step 01

Apply online in under 10 minutes

Fill a simple application with your basic business details and KYC information. Entirely digital — no branch visits, no paperwork, no phone calls.

Step 02

Upload documents digitally

Upload business financials, KYC documents, and collateral details through our secure portal. Scanned copies accepted — no originals needed.

Step 03

Credit assessment with transparent criteria

Our credit team evaluates your application using clearly-defined criteria. You're informed at every stage. No black boxes, no unexplained rejections.

Step 04

Funds in your account within 7 days

Upon approval, the loan amount is credited directly to your bank account. Average turnaround from application to disbursal is just 7 working days.
